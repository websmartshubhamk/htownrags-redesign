# Blog Page - Detailed Specification

## Page Overview
Content hub for vintage fashion insights, style guides, brand stories, and community news.

## Sections Breakdown

### 1. Hero Section
**Content**:
- Page title: "The Vintage Journal"
- Subtitle: "Stories, Style, and Sustainable Fashion"
- Featured article slider
- Newsletter signup prompt

### 2. Blog Categories
**Purpose**: Content organization

**Categories**:
- Style Guides
- Brand Spotlights
- Vintage History
- Sustainability
- Store News
- How To & DIY
- Trend Reports
- Community Features

**UI**: Horizontal scrollable pills or dropdown

### 3. Featured Articles Section
**Purpose**: Highlight important content

**Layout**: 
- Main featured article (large card)
- 2-3 secondary featured posts
- Visual hierarchy with images
- Read time indicators
- Author attribution

### 4. Recent Posts Grid
**Purpose**: Latest content display

**Card Components**:
- Thumbnail image
- Category tag
- Title
- Excerpt (2-3 lines)
- Author and date
- Read time
- Comment count
- Share preview

**Layout**: 3-column responsive grid

### 5. Sidebar Elements
**Purpose**: Additional engagement

**Components**:
- Search blog
- Popular posts
- Categories cloud
- Tags
- Newsletter signup
- Social media links
- Recent comments
- Archive by month

### 6. Individual Article Features
**Purpose**: Enhanced reading experience

**Article Page Elements**:
- Hero image
- Breadcrumbs
- Share buttons (floating)
- Table of contents (long posts)
- Author bio box
- Related posts
- Comments section
- Print-friendly version

### 7. Interactive Elements
**Purpose**: Increase engagement

**Features**:
- Like/bookmark articles
- Email subscription per category
- Social sharing
- Comment system
- Article ratings
- Style quiz/polls

### 8. Content Types
**Purpose**: Varied content formats

**Formats**:
- Long-form articles
- Photo essays
- Video content
- Infographics
- Interviews
- Guest posts
- Buying guides
- Event recaps
