# Homepage - Detailed Specification

## Page Overview
The homepage serves as the main entry point showcasing H-Town Rags' vintage clothing collection and brand values.

## Sections Breakdown

### 1. Hero Section
**Purpose**: Immediate brand impact and value proposition

**Content**:
- Headline: "Authentic Vintage Clothing Since [Year]"
- Subheadline: "Discover timeless pieces from iconic brands"
- CTA Button: "Shop Collection" 
- Secondary CTA: "Our Story"
- Background: Full-width vintage clothing collage or video

**Features**:
- Parallax scrolling effect
- Auto-playing video option (muted)
- Mobile-optimized layout

### 2. Featured Categories
**Purpose**: Quick navigation to popular product categories

**Layout**: 6-grid responsive layout
- Denim
- Workwear  
- Sportswear
- Band Tees
- USA Vintage
- New Arrivals

**Features**:
- Hover effects showing subcategories
- Quick shop preview
- Category image backgrounds

### 3. Brand Story Section
**Purpose**: Build trust and connection

**Content**:
- "Why Choose H-Town Rags" heading
- 3 key pillars:
  - Authenticity (genuine vintage pieces)
  - Sustainability (eco-friendly fashion)
  - Community (local Hitchin business)
- Link to full About page

### 4. Featured Products Carousel
**Purpose**: Showcase best sellers and new arrivals

**Features**:
- Auto-scrolling product slider
- Quick view functionality
- Add to wishlist button
- Price and size availability
- "New" and "Sale" badges

### 5. Brand Showcase
**Purpose**: Highlight premium brands carried

**Brands Grid**:
- Levi's, Carhartt, Nike, Adidas
- Champion, Dickies, Ralph Lauren
- The North Face, Lee, Wrangler
- Interactive brand filtering

### 6. Instagram Feed Integration
**Purpose**: Social proof and community engagement

**Features**:
- Latest 8 Instagram posts
- Shoppable posts functionality
- Follow CTA
- User-generated content

### 7. Newsletter Signup
**Purpose**: Build email list for marketing

**Design**:
- Floating bar or modal popup
- "Get 10% off your first order"
- Email field with vintage-styled submit button
- Privacy policy link

### 8. Testimonials
**Purpose**: Build trust through social proof

**Content**:
- 3-4 customer reviews
- Google Reviews integration
- Star ratings display
- Rotating testimonials

### 9. Store Information
**Purpose**: Drive foot traffic to physical store

**Content**:
- Store hours
- Location with map preview
- "Visit Us" CTA
- Contact information

## Technical Requirements
- Lazy loading for images
- SEO optimized meta tags
- Schema markup for local business
- Performance budget: <3s load time
- Accessibility: WCAG 2.1 AA

## Mobile Considerations
- Hamburger menu navigation
- Touch-friendly carousels
- Optimized image sizes
- Simplified grid layouts
- Thumb-friendly CTAs
