# About Page - Detailed Specification

## Page Overview
The About page tells the H-Town Rags story, showcases the team, and communicates brand values.

## Sections Breakdown

### 1. Hero Section
**Content**:
- Page title: "Our Story"
- Hero image: Store front or team photo
- Brief tagline about the brand's mission

### 2. Brand Story
**Purpose**: Connect with customers through storytelling

**Content Structure**:
- Founding story and year established
- Evolution of the business
- Mission statement
- Vision for sustainable fashion

**Visual Elements**:
- Timeline component showing milestones
- Historical photos
- Founder quotes

### 3. Our Values
**Purpose**: Communicate brand principles

**Four Core Values**:
1. **Authenticity**
   - Genuine vintage pieces
   - Careful curation process
   
2. **Sustainability**
   - Environmental impact
   - Circular fashion economy
   
3. **Community**
   - Local Hitchin roots
   - Supporting local causes
   
4. **Quality**
   - Hand-picked selection
   - Condition standards

### 4. Meet the Team
**Purpose**: Build personal connection

**Layout**: Team member cards with:
- Photo
- Name and role
- Fun fact about vintage fashion
- Favorite vintage piece

### 5. Our Process
**Purpose**: Build trust through transparency

**Steps**:
1. Sourcing (where items come from)
2. Authentication (verification process)
3. Restoration (cleaning and repairs)
4. Curation (selection criteria)
5. Presentation (in-store and online)

**Visual**: Illustrated process diagram

### 6. Sustainability Commitment
**Purpose**: Appeal to eco-conscious consumers

**Content**:
- Environmental statistics
- Circular fashion benefits
- Carbon footprint reduction
- Packaging information

### 7. Press & Recognition
**Purpose**: Build credibility

**Features**:
- Media mentions
- Awards received
- Customer testimonials
- Press kit download

### 8. Store History
**Purpose**: Local connection

**Content**:
- The Carling Building history
- Coopers Yard community
- Hitchin heritage
- Photo gallery

## Visual Design
- Vintage photo filters
- Newspaper-style layouts
- Hand-drawn elements
- Authentic photography

## Technical Requirements
- Smooth scroll animations
- Image optimization
- SEO-friendly URLs
- Fast loading times

## Mobile Optimization
- Stacked layouts
- Touch-friendly timeline
- Optimized team photos
- Readable typography
