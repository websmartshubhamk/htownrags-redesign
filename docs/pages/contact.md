# Contact Page - Detailed Specification

## Page Overview
Comprehensive contact hub providing multiple communication channels and essential business information.

## Sections Breakdown

### 1. Hero Section
**Content**:
- Page title: "Get in Touch"
- Subtitle: "Visit our store or reach out online"
- Hero image: Storefront or team photo
- Quick contact buttons: Call | Email | Visit

### 2. Contact Methods Grid
**Purpose**: Multiple contact options

**Three Columns**:
1. **Visit Us**
   - Full address
   - Opening hours
   - Parking information
   - Public transport links
   - "Get Directions" button

2. **Call Us**
   - Phone number (clickable)
   - Best times to call
   - WhatsApp integration
   - "Call Now" button

3. **Email Us**
   - General inquiries email
   - Response time expectation
   - "Send Email" button
   - Contact form link

### 3. Interactive Map Section
**Purpose**: Store location visualization

**Features**:
- Embedded Google Maps

- Directions integration
- Parking locations marked
- Public transport stops
